About Bhuvana Enterprises

Established in 2017, Bhuvana Enterprises has gained extensive experience in industrial flooring applications over 8 years. We are the leading contractor, manufacturer, and authorized dealer for acid-resistant tiles, granite bedding, and specialized industrial flooring solutions in Chennai and across South India.

  • 8+ Years of Industry Experience

  • 30+ Major Projects Completed

  • Certified Materials (IS 4457, IS 4860, BS 2050)

  • Pan-India Service Coverage

Our Industrial Flooring Services

Comprehensive manufacturing and installation solutions for specialized industrial flooring
applications across pharmaceutical, chemical, and manufacturing industries.

Manufacturing

In-house production of high-quality acid resistant tiles, bricks, and specialized flooring materials with stringent quality control measures.

Installation & Contracting

Expert installation services by trained professionals ensuring precise application and long-lasting performance of industrial flooring systems.

Consultation & Design

Technical consultation for flooring requirements, site assessment, custom solution design, and material selection guidance.

Quality Testing

Project Management
